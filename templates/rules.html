Rules & Regulations

Please read carefully before registering. Rule violations will result in immediate disqualification.

01

Tournament Rules

Use of hacks, cheats, or unfair play will lead to team disqualification. Late entry will not allowed once the match starts. Room ID pass will be shared 10 minutes before the match. Players must use the same ID registered. Entry Fees : 699 per squad. Registration fee is non-refundable.

02

Game Rules

Total 5 matches (Round 1-3 matches, Final - 2 matches). First round will be the elimination round. Qualification will be based on total points (rank +kills). Only mobile devices are allowed.

Mobile Phones Allowed Emulators/iPads Banned
03

Match Timings & Lobbies

Room ID and Password will be shared precisely 15 minutes before the match start time in the designated WhatsApp verification group. The match will start strictly on time. Latecomers will not be entertained.

04

Anti-Cheat & Third Party Software

The use of aimbots, wallhacks, speed hacks, GFX tools altering game files, or any third-party software that gives an unfair advantage is strictly forbidden.
